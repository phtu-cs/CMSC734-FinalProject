Dota2 Pro Strategy

Authors: Duotun Wang, Yi Liu, Yixuan Ren, Peihan Tu

In this project, we will research the problem of how the big data of the multiplayer video game (Dota2) affect the strategies made by teams and players. Further to this, we aim at producing a detailed interactive analysis to help users pick heroes, items, and win team fights.
